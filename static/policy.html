Secure Chat Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 31, 2025

Thank you for using Secure Chat. Your privacy and data security are of the utmost importance to us. This Privacy Policy outlines the information we collect, how we use and safeguard it, and your rights in relation to your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

  • Registration Information: Username, encrypted private key
  • Communication Data: Encrypted messages and timestamps
  • Technical and Usage Data: App version, device details, crash reports
  • Cookies and Storage: Session and local storage data

2. Use of Information

  • Authenticate accounts
  • Transmit encrypted communications
  • Analyze performance anonymously
  • Assist with support

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

  • We use end-to-end encryption; no access to message content
  • No selling or renting of personal data
  • Legal requests are honored with minimum disclosure

4. Data Retention

  • Encrypted messages stored until delivery or 30 days
  • Account data retained until deletion
  • Crash reports retained for 90 days

5. Security Measures

  • RSA-2048 + AES-256 hybrid encryption
  • All data transmitted over HTTPS
  • Private keys never sent in plaintext
  • Server infrastructure hardened and updated regularly

6. Children’s Privacy

Secure Chat is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

7. Your Rights

  • Access and correct your account data
  • Delete your account anytime
  • Export encrypted messages before deletion

8. Policy Updates

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted here and reflected in-app for major updates.
